How it works

Your prototypes, ready to share

ProtoVid automatically detects your prototype flows and exports them as high-quality videos. No setup required.

1

Open the plugin

Run ProtoVid from the Figma plugins menu on any page with prototype connections.

2

Click Export

ProtoVid scans your prototype graph, captures every frame, and encodes the video.

3

Download & share

Get an MP4 file you can drop into Slack, Notion, Google Slides, or anywhere else.
